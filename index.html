Pickup & Attack Demo (10 hits to die)

Player 1 (Blue): W/A/S/D to move, F to pick up / use. Player 2 (Red): Arrow keys to move, L to pick up / use.

Controls

  • Player 1 (Blue): Move with W/A/S/D. Pick up / use item with F.
  • Player 2 (Red): Move with Arrow Keys. Pick up / use item with L.

Objective: Pick up items, use them to damage the other player. Each player has 10 HP; when HP reaches 0 the player dies and becomes a spectator.
